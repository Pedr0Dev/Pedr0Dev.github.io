Xiaomi launches Redmi cellphone without charger in the box for the 1st time.
After criticizing Apple for removing the charger from the box of its cell phones, Xiaomi did not hesitate to launch its top of the line in 2020 without the accessory included.
Maykon Lafaette 12/05/2019
New Redmi mobile launched by Xiaomi without charger. (Photo: Xiaomi)
The Chinese manufacturer Xiaomi has led a controversial market trend for the Redmi cell phone line. For the first time, the brand launched a smartphone of the line without a charger in the box. The device in question is the Redmi Note 11SE, which was presented by the company in India recently. According to GSM Arena, the model only has a USB-C cable in its packaging, leaving out the charging source.
The company has launched cell phones without a charger in the box since the Mi 11, but the company tends to carry out the practice only in its main line. The Redmi family of devices already had a model with an optional charger, the K40, but never a model that only had a USB cable in the box. It is worth mentioning that the company has not yet confirmed whether other phones in the Redmi line will also come without a charger. In the case of the Indian market, only the Redmi Note 11SE is being sold without the power supply in the box, points out GSM Arena.
New cellphone with its box going to the market. (Photo: Xiaomi)
In addition to the charger controversy, the Redmi Note 11SE can also cause annoyance because of its specifications. The model has a technical sheet similar to the Redmi Note 10S, released last year. With a 6.43-inch Super Amoled screen, the model has a Helio G95 chipset, up to 64 MP cameras and a 5,000 mAh battery. The product also supports 33W fast charging, but you need to have the charger or buy it separately to enjoy the speed. The launch of Redmi Note 11SE is scheduled for tomorrow (26) in India. The arrival of the device in Brazil, as well as its price, is still unconfirmed. "I was analyzing it and I was afraid of how the public would react. But I think this will be a new adaptation for the market with improvements." Said the president of Xiaomi.
